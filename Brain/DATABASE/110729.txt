Birds are amazing creatures that come in all shapes and sizes, with over 10,000 species found across the globe.

They're essential to the environment, playing crucial roles in pollination, seed dispersal, and insect control.

Here are some fun facts about birds:

    They have feathers, which are unique to them.

Feathers are lightweight and strong, helping birds fly, stay warm, and waterproof themselves.

They have hollow bones, which make them lighter for flight.

They have excellent eyesight, and some species can even see ultraviolet light.

They can navigate long distances using the Earth's magnetic field.

Some species can mimic sounds, including human speech.
